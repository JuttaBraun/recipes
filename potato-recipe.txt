500 g potatoes
a little salt
50 g grated cheese
(Some remoulade) 

Peel the potatoes and cut it into smal cubes.
Put it on a plate and cook it a microwave for 8 minutues (600 Watt).
Turn the potatoes and cook for another 8 minutues in the mircowave.
Put a bit salt on it and strew the cheese on it.
If you like put some remoulade on top.
